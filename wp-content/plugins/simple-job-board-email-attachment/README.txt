== Description ==

= Are you looking for applicant resume & details as an email attachment in Job board plugin? = 
Simple Job Board Email Application by <a href="http://www.presstigers.com">PressTigers</a> is an easy and light weight add-on that send the applicant details and uploaded resume to Admin & HR via email. This plugin is focused on extendibility and ease of use. You can directly receive the applicant details & resume in email. By using this add-on, you can get rid of website login to view applicants request. You can also Enable/Disbale the receiving of applicant details & resume attachment in email through global Settings.

= Plugin Features =

* Send uploaded resume to HR, Admin & Job Author via email.
* Send applicant details to HR, Admin & Job Author via email.
* Send uploaded resume & applicant details to multiple HR email addresses.
* Enable/Disable resume attachment for HR, Admin & Job Author through global settings.	
* Enable/Disable attachment of applicant's details for HR, Admin & Job Author through global settings.

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin add-on through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. It automatically adds settings for email attachment of applicant details & resume for Admin and HR under Email Notification tab.
